Tasting Notes

Appellation: North Coast / Mount St. Helena – Lake County

Composition: Majority Clone 7 Cabernet Sauvignon, with Clone 8, a splash of Clone 4, Cabernet Franc, and Petite Sirah

Appearance

Deep garnet with a vivid ruby rim, showcasing exceptional clarity and intensity.

Nose

Crushed rose petal and plum compote, with layers of ripe blackcurrant, blackberry compote, and dark cherry. Subtle earthy undertones emerge, mingling with hints of smoked meat, and a delicate touch of dried herbs. Notes of graphite and cocoa add complexity.

Palate

Concentrated and structured, with velvety tannins and a robust profile. Clone 7 provides lush black fruit, Clone 8 contributes finesse and balance, Clone 4 adds aromatic lift and savory nuances, and the Petite Sirah deepens the intensity with its signature black pepper and plum notes. A faint leesy character introduces a savory, umami quality, complementing the wine’s meaty edge.

Finish

Long and persistent, with lingering flavors of dark chocolate, espresso, and a subtle minerality. The wine is rich yet balanced, with a vibrancy that promises excellent aging potential.

Pairing Suggestions

Grilled ribeye, lamb chops with rosemary, or a hearty mushroom risotto. For cheese, try aged Gouda or Taleggio to enhance the wine’s savory elements.

“Best paired with a three-night stay at the Mt. St. Helena Ranch Airbnb.”

Technical Details

Varietal: Red Wine Blend, majority Clone 7 Cabernet Sauvignon

Aging: 12 months in French Oak (~10% American Oak)

Best From: 2025–2032

Analysis:

  • TA: 6.15 g/L
  • pH: 3.72
  • Alcohol: 14.4%
